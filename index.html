About the Artist

Douglas George is a retired construction engineer whose lifelong passion for art has blossomed into a rewarding second chapter. Throughout his working years, he dedicated himself to learning the craft of drawing and painting, developing a keen eye for capturing the beauty of landscapes, wildlife, and human subjects.

Through numerous workshops and dedicated practice, Doug has developed skill with the delicate balance of watercolor and the rich textures of acrylic paint. His work celebrates the natural world, inviting viewers to appreciate the subtle details and quiet moments often overlooked in our busy lives.

Doug's art stems from a deep appreciation for the world around us - the way light falls on a forest path, the harmony of colors in a coastal scene, or the character in an animal's expression. As a firm believer that everyone possesses natural creative talents, he encourages others to discover the joy that artistic expression can bring to life.

All of his paintings are created with acrylic on canvas and are available framed or unframed.
