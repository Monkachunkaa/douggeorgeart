About the Artist

Douglas George is a retired construction engineer. Throughout his working years, he took time to learn how to draw and compose artistic images of animals, people and nature. He has attended numerous workshops and has become skilled in the use of watercolor and acrylic paint. He enjoys art as it has increased his appreciation of nature, people and animals. George is a firm believer in everyone learning to use their natural talents for the joy it can bring to life.
